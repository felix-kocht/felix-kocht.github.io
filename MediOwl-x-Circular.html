MediOwl x Circular Hackfest

After TMS our team was on the hunt for a new hackathon to challenge us and help us further refine our prototype.

Because of this, our tech team join the Cirucular Hackfest, participating on the health challenge, where it was requested to develop a device that could monitor certain biomarkers powered by solar energy.

This was an amazing opportunity for our team not to only reduce our current prototype but also to learn about different technologies and how the application of solar based technologies could make our product more sustainable.

We are extremely grateful with Panasonic, Michael Spunt and the Circular Hackfest Organizers for giving us the opportunity for participating in this exciting event. Looking forward to our next Hackathon!
